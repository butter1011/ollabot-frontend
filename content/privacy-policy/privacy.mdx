## Introduction

At Ollabot we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy aims to inform you about the practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of information you may provide through our website.

## Data Collection

### Personal Data

When using our site, you may be required to provide personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number during the creation of a user account, subscription to our newsletter, or while using our services.

### Navigation Data

We also collect information related to your use of the site, such as your IP address, the pages visited, and connection times through the use of cookies and similar technologies.

## Data Use

Your data is collected to enable:

- The provision and improvement of our services;
- The management of your account and requests;
- The sending of marketing communications subject to your consent;
- The conduct of statistical analyses.

## Data Sharing

We commit to not selling, renting, sharing, or disclosing your personal data to third parties except in the following cases:

- Service providers acting on our behalf;
- Compliance with legal obligations to respond to a court decision or legal process.

## Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Your Rights

In accordance with applicable law, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, and oppose the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address: contact@ollabot.com.
