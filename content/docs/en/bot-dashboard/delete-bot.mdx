This guide will walk you through the steps to delete your chatbot on Ollabot. Deleting a chatbot is a straightforward process, but it's important to ensure you truly want to remove it, as this action cannot be undone.

## Deleting a Chatbot

You can delete your chatbot by following these steps. Make sure you confirm the deletion by typing the required confirmation phrase.

### Steps to Delete Your Chatbot

1. **Log into Ollabot:** Start by logging into your Ollabot account. Navigate to the bot dashboard.
2. **Select Your Bot:** Choose the bot you want to delete.
3. **Go to the Delete Bot Section:** In the bot dashboard, find the "Delete Bot" section.

### Confirming Deletion

To ensure that the deletion process is intentional and to avoid accidental deletions, you will need to confirm your action by typing the confirmation phrase.

1. **Type Confirmation Phrase:** To confirm the deletion, type the phrase "Delete Chatbot" in the provided text field.

<Image src="/images/bot-dashboard/delete-chatbot.png" width="718" height="404" alt="Confirm Delete Chatbot" />

### Finalizing Deletion

Once you have entered the required information, proceed with the deletion:

1. **Click Delete Button:** Click on the "Delete Chatbot" button to proceed.
2. **Wait for Confirmation:** A notification will appear confirming that the chatbot has been successfully deleted.

## Important Considerations

Before deleting your chatbot, consider the following:

- **Irreversible Action:** Deleting a chatbot is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you have backed up any important data.
- **User Impact:** If your chatbot is actively used by customers or users, ensure that its deletion will not disrupt their experience.

## Conclusion

Deleting a chatbot on Ollabot is a simple yet significant action. Make sure you have considered all the implications before proceeding. By following this guide, you can ensure that the deletion process is handled smoothly and efficiently.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to Ollabot support for help.

Utilize these steps to manage your chatbot effectively, ensuring a smooth and seamless experience for your users.
