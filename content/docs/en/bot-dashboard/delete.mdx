# How to Delete Your Chatbot on Ollabot

Follow these steps to delete your chatbot on Ollabot:

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Access the Chatbot Dashboard

Log into your Ollabot account and navigate to the dashboard.

### 2. Select Your Bot

From the sidebar, select the bot you want to delete. This will take you to the bot's settings page.

### 3. Navigate to Delete Option

In the bot settings, find the "Delete Chatbot" option. This is typically located in the sidebar.

<Image src="/images/bot-dashboard/delete.png"

### 4. Confirm Deletion

Follow the instructions to confirm the deletion. You may need to type a confirmation text to verify your action. 

<Image src="/images/bot-dashboard/delete-confirm.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>

### 5. Submit the Form

Click the delete button to finalize the deletion of your chatbot. The system will process your request, and your bot will be deleted.
