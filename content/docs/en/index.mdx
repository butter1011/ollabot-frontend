Welcome to the documentation for Ollabot, a comprehensive AI chatbot platform designed to enhance customer interactions and automate workflows.

## What is Ollabot?

Ollabot is an advanced AI chatbot platform that allows you to create, customize, and integrate chatbots into various platforms and websites. Ollabot leverages AI to provide intelligent responses, automate customer service, and improve user engagement.

## Key Features

### Customization

Ollabot provides extensive customization options to tailor the chatbot's appearance and behavior to match your brand. Customize the bot's name, avatar, welcome messages, and conversation tone.

### Integrations

Seamlessly integrate Ollabot with popular platforms like Bubble, Framer, Shopify, Webflow, Weebly, Wix, and WordPress. Embedding your chatbot into these platforms is straightforward and enhances your customer interactions.

## Getting Started

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up for an Ollabot account to start building your chatbot.
2. **Configure Your Bot**: Upload relevant data sources such as files, text snippets, websites, or question-and-answer pairs to build the chatbot's knowledge base.
3. **Customize Your Bot**: Use the bot dashboard to customize the appearance and behavior of your chatbot.
4. **Integrate with Platforms**: Embed your chatbot on your website or application using the provided integration guides.
5. **Use the API**: Interact with your chatbot programmatically using Ollabot's API.
