## Features

- **Questions Answered:** The number of questions your bot can answer per month.
- **Data Sources:** The number of different data sources you can integrate with your bot.
- **Community Support:** Access to community forums and support.
- **Analytics and Insight Dashboard:** Get insights and analytics about your bot's performance.
- **Premium Email Support:** Priority email support from our team.
- **Real-time Update from Data Source:** Your bot's knowledge base will update in real-time with changes to the data source.
- **API Access:** Access to Ollabot's API for deeper integration.
- **Remove 'Powered by Bot Generator':** Option to remove the "Powered by Bot Generator" branding from your chatbot.
