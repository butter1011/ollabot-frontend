## How to Join the Affiliate Program

Follow these steps to join the Ollabot Affiliate Program:

### Step 1: Visit the Affiliate Program Page

Visit this link: [ollabot.promotekit.com](https://ollabot.promotekit.com)

### Step 2: Sign Up

Sign up using your email. 

![Affiliate Signup](/images/affiliate/affiliate_signup.png)

You will receive a verification code, which you will need to enter to complete the sign-up process.

![Affiliate Code](/images/affiliate/affiliate_code.png)

### Step 3: Fill Out the Form

After signing up, fill out the form with the required details, including your PayPal email for receiving payments.

![Affiliate Form](/images/affiliate/affiliate_form.png)

### Step 4: Access the Dashboard

![Affiliate Dashboard](/images/affiliate/affiliate_dashboard.png)

Once you have completed the form, you will be redirected to the dashboard where you can see your affiliate link.

## Conclusion

By following these steps, you can easily join the Ollabot Affiliate Program and start earning commissions by referring new customers.
