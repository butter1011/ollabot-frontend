## Step 1: Set Up Your Ollabot Chatbot

To integrate your Ollabot AI chatbot into your telegram bot, start by Generate your telegram bot token.
Follow this step-by-step roadmap for suaccessfully deploying your Ollabot chatbot.
1. Find botFather in telegram. send "/start" to start.

<Image src="/images/integrations/telegram-app1.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>
2. Enter "/newbot" and set bot name and user name. Then you will see your telegram bot token.


<Image src="/images/integrations/telegram-app2.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>
3. Copy your telegram bot token to clipboard
4. To customize your telegram bot, enter "/mybots", select your bot and click "Edit bot" button.

<Image src="/images/integrations/telegram-set.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>
  In this panel, you can set name, description, picture of Bot.
## Step 2: Launch your telegram bot

1. Each chatbot created on Ollabot has a unique embed code for integration. Once your chatbot is set up on Ollabot, navigate to your Dashboard page and select the specific bot you want to integrate with your Bubble application.

<Image src="/images/integrations/dashboard.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>
2. Clicking on the bot will take you to its preview page. From there, go to the "Embed on Site" tab, and click the "Make Public" button.

3. A pop-up window will appear with the provided HTML code. Click "Copy Script" to copy the code snippet.

<Image src="/images/integrations/embed.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>

4. On your dashboard, select the Launch Editor" button next to it.

<Image src="/images/integrations/telegram-embed.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>

6.  Test your telegram telegram to test your chatbot.

<Image src="/images/integrations/telegram-test.png" 
  width="718" 
  height="404" 
  alt="Image" 
/>

Congratulations, your Ollabot chatbot is now live on your telegram app!
